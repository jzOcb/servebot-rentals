Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own tennis balls?

The machine holds 150 balls. You can bring your own or add a can of balls for +$5 when booking.

How does the deposit work?

We charge a $300 refundable deposit via Stripe. Once you return the machine undamaged, the deposit is refunded within 24 hours.

Is the machine difficult to use?

Not at all! We provide a quick tutorial video, and you can also get a demo when picking up. Most people are ready to play in 5 minutes.

Can I use it on any tennis court?

Yes! The machine is battery-powered, so no outlet needed. Works on public courts, club courts, or your own court.

Where is the pickup location?

Pickup is in Newton, MA. Exact address provided after booking confirmation. We also offer delivery within 15 miles for +$25.

What if the machine has issues during my rental?

Contact us immediately and we'll troubleshoot or arrange a replacement. Your satisfaction is guaranteed.
